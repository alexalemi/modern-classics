The pain of the burning forced her to wake, though she had been sleeping soundly. The scholar, hearing her weakness in her voice and seeing her body burned by the sun, was moved to a little compassion. "Alas, wretch that I am!" cried the lady. "May God grant such beauty, acquired in this manner, to those who wish me ill!
