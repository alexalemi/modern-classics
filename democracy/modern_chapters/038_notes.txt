- The patriotism passage ("The Union is a vast body...") -- this is one of Tocqueville's most famous miscalculations. Translated faithfully; the editorial note at the top already tells readers he was wrong about this.
- The state vs. federal government comparison (tone of voice when addressing individuals vs. states) -- this is a brilliant observational passage. Kept it vivid.
- Jefferson quotation about the War of 1812 -- kept and attributed clearly.
