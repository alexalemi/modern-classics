One August, Fra Cipolla made his usual annual visit to the town. On a Sunday morning, he gathered the local villagers and announced he had a special treat for them: he would show them an actual feather from the Angel Gabriel - a relic he'd brought back from his travels.

Little did they know, Fra Cipolla was about to turn their prank completely on its head.
