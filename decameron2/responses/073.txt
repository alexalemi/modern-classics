The Fourth Story: A Wife's Clever Revenge

Love is wild and unpredictable! Who could ever imagine the incredible tricks people play when they're desperate? No philosopher or expert could dream up the brilliant strategies that love inspires in people.

In the city of Arezzo, there lived a wealthy man named Tofano, who was married to a beautiful woman named Ghita. For no good reason, Tofano became intensely jealous of his wife. When Ghita tried to understand why he was so suspicious, he could never give her a straight answer.

Frustrated by his groundless jealousy, Ghita decided to give her husband something real to worry about. She noticed a young man who was clearly interested in her and carefully began to connect with him. Knowing her husband loved drinking, she started encouraging his alcoholic habits, often pushing him to drink until he was completely wasted.

Her plan worked perfectly. She'd get Tofano so drunk that he'd pass out, and then she'd spend time with her lover - sometimes even bringing him to their house or spending part of the night at his place. Eventually, Tofano caught on that his wife never drank while pushing him to get drunk.

One evening, Tofano pretended to be extremely drunk, hoping to catch his wife in the act. As soon as she thought he was passed out, Ghita left to meet her lover. When she returned, Tofano had locked all the doors.

"You're not getting back in," he announced. "I know exactly what you've been doing."

Ghita tried begging, then threatening. "If you don't let me in," she said, "I'll throw myself down the well. Everyone will think you killed me because of your drunkenness!"

When Tofano ran to stop her from supposedly jumping, Ghita slipped back into the house and locked him out. From the window, she mocked him: "Next time, water your wine while you're drinking - not after!"

The neighbors woke up to their shouting. Ghita brilliantly turned the situation around, telling everyone that Tofano was a drunk who came home at all hours. The neighbors sided with her, and her family gave Tofano such a beating that he was barely left standing.

In the end, Tofano had to make peace. He promised never to be jealous again and basically gave his wife permission to do whatever she wanted - as long as she was discreet.

Love wins again! Take that, jealousy!
