The Fifth Story: How Calandrino Got Totally Played

Let me tell you about Calandrino, a painter who was about to have the most embarrassing romantic adventure of his life.

It all started at a construction site where Calandrino was working with his painter buddies Bruno, Buffalmacco, and Nello. The worksite belonged to Niccolò Cornacchini, and his son Filippo occasionally used the place to entertain his girlfriends.

One day, Calandrino spotted Niccolosa - a woman who worked as... well, let's just say she was available for hire - washing up in the courtyard. She was dressed casually, her hair pulled back, looking pretty attractive. Calandrino immediately fell head over heels.

"Dude," he told his friend Bruno, "I'm totally in love! This woman is incredible!"

Bruno, who loved messing with Calandrino, decided to turn this into an epic prank. He contacted Filippo and Niccolosa, and they hatched a plan to completely string Calandrino along.

For two months, they played with Calandrino's emotions. He'd bring gifts, sing love songs, and basically make a total fool of himself. Bruno would pretend to pass messages from Niccolosa, giving Calandrino just enough hope to keep him excited.

Finally, Bruno told Calandrino he had a "magical" solution - a special script that would make Niccolosa instantly fall for him. All Calandrino needed to do was touch her with this "magical" parchment.

When the moment came, Calandrino followed the instructions exactly. He touched Niccolosa, who immediately led him to a barn. But instead of a romantic encounter, she basically just teased him mercilessly.

The real drama started when Calandrino's wife, Tessa, showed up. Guided by a tip from Nello, she burst into the barn and went absolutely ballistic. She scratched Calandrino's face, pulled his hair, and unleashed a tornado of fury.

"You worthless cheater!" she screamed. "I'll teach you to mess around!"

Calandrino's painter friends watched, trying not to laugh, as his wife completely destroyed him. In the end, he was sent home humiliated - covered in scratches, totally embarrassed, and learning a painful lesson about trying to cheat on his wife.

The moral? Don't believe everything your friends tell you - especially when it involves "magical" love scripts.
