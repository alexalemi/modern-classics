But Ansaldo was no ordinary admirer. He tracked down a magical practitioner who promised to create the impossible garden - for a hefty price. When Dianora saw the garden, she was stunned. She had never expected Ansaldo to actually fulfill her impossible request. Worried about the promise she'd made, she confessed everything to her husband.
