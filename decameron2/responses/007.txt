How a Brilliant Hostess Shut Down a King's Inappropriate Crush

King Philippe, who hadn't even met her, immediately became obsessed. He decided he absolutely had to meet this legendary woman. Under the excuse of traveling to join the crusade, he planned a detour to visit her while her husband was away.

When the king announced he was coming to dinner, the Marchioness was no fool. She knew exactly what was going on - this was a thinly veiled attempt to seduce her. But instead of panicking, she got clever.

She instructed her staff to prepare a meal using only chickens. Every single dish: chicken this, chicken that. When the king arrived, everything was elegant and perfectly prepared - but entirely chicken-based.

During dinner, the king couldn't help but notice. Raising an eyebrow, he asked her slyly, "Are there no roosters in this region?"

Without missing a beat, the Marchioness replied, "My lord, women might look different and hold various social positions, but fundamentally, we're all the same."

Her subtle message was clear: Just because you're a king doesn't mean you can have whatever - or whoever - you want.

The king immediately understood. Embarrassed and shut down, he finished his all-chicken meal, thanked her for her hospitality, and left quickly - his inappropriate intentions thoroughly defeated by her wit.
