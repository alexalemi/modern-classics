The Revenge of the Hungry Man

In Florence, there lived a guy everyone called Ciacco - basically the city's most legendary foodie. Problem was, his wallet couldn't keep up with his massive appetite. But Ciacco was smart: instead of starving, he'd charm his way into fancy dinners, hanging out with rich folks who loved good food.

Around the same time, there was this other guy named Biondello - a total smooth operator who dressed like he was always ready for a photoshoot, with perfectly styled hair and clothes that screamed "I'm cool." He played the same game as Ciacco: getting free meals through pure charm.

One morning during Lent, Biondello was checking out some seriously nice lampreys at the fish market, planning to buy them for a wealthy friend, Messer Vieri. Ciacco spotted him and asked what was up.

"These lampreys?" Biondello grinned. "They're actually for Messer Corso Donati. He's got even better fish at home and wants these extras for a dinner party. Wanna come?"

Ciacco, never one to miss a potential feast, said, "Absolutely!"

When Ciacco showed up at Messer Corso's place, he was expecting a epic meal. Instead, he got a few chickpeas, some pickled fish, and basically nothing else. Biondello had totally played him.

Furious but crafty, Ciacco decided on revenge. He hired a street vendor and sent him with a message to Messer Filippo Argenti - known as the most hot-tempered guy in Florence. The vendor was to deliver a bizarre message about "rubifying" a wine flask for Biondello's "minions."

Messer Filippo, known for his explosive temper, went ballistic. When Biondello showed up, Filippo absolutely destroyed him - punching him, throwing him in the mud, ripping his clothes. Poor Biondello had no idea what was happening.

Later, when they crossed paths, Ciacco couldn't help but laugh. "How'd you enjoy Messer Filippo's wine?" he teased.

Biondello, bruised and humiliated, shot back, "About as much as you enjoyed Messer Corso's lampreys."

From that day on, Biondello learned a crucial lesson: never mess with a hungry man who's good at plotting revenge.
