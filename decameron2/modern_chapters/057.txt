"You're home early," she said.

"Well, something interesting happened at dinner," Pietro replied. He then told her a story about his friend Ercolano catching another man hidden in his house - basically, the exact same scenario she was currently experiencing.

But here's where the story gets truly bizarre. Instead of being angry, Pietro was actually thrilled - because this particular young man was someone HE had been pursuing for his own... let's say "personal interests."

What followed was a surprisingly pragmatic negotiation. The wife tried to justify her actions, Pietro seemed more interested in having dinner, and somehow, they all ended up eating together.

By morning, the young man had been "escorted" back to town, with everyone involved having their own complicated version of what exactly happened that night.

The moral? Be careful what you wish for - and sometimes, karma has a seriously twisted sense of humor.
