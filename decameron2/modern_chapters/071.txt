Peronella, cool as can be, whispers to Giannello, "Quick, hide in that big storage vat! My husband's back, and this is NOT good."

What follows is a hilarious sequence where Giannello plays along, claiming the vat needs cleaning. The husband, eager to please, starts scrubbing the vat while Giannello... well, let's just say he takes full advantage of the situation with Peronella, who's "helping" her husband clean.
