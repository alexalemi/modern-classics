Let me tell you a story that'll break your heart—a tale of love, betrayal, and revenge that shows just how dangerous jealousy can be.

Spoiler alert: They weren't.

After she finished, he dropped the bomb. "You just ate the heart of the man you were sleeping with," he told her.

Her response? Total horror. She realized what he'd done and, in a moment of pure desperation, threw herself out of a high window, dying instantly beside her lover's memory.

The locals buried them together in the same tomb, a final testament to a love that destroyed everything in its path.

Sometimes passion comes with a deadly price.
