The Tenth Story

Two friends from Siena had a wild adventure that you won't believe. Here's how it went down.

In Siena, there were two young guys named Tingoccio Mini and Meuccio di Tura who were basically inseparable. They were so close that they made this crazy pact: whoever died first would come back and tell the other what the afterlife was really like.

Now, Tingoccio became godfather to a child belonging to a guy named Ambruogio. During these visits, he met Ambruogio's wife, Mistress Mita - and wow, was she gorgeous. Tingoccio fell hard for her, even though she was technically his godchild's mother. 

But here's the twist: Meuccio also had a thing for her. Neither of them told the other about their crush, but for different reasons. Tingoccio was embarrassed about wanting his friend's child's mother. Meuccio knew Tingoccio already liked her and didn't want to cause drama.

Eventually, Tingoccio managed to hook up with Mistress Mita. He got a bit too enthusiastic and caught some kind of illness that killed him. But true to their pact, he came back from the dead three days later to fill Meuccio in on the afterlife.

Meuccio was freaked out but curious. "Are you in hell?" he asked.

"Not exactly," Tingoccio replied. "I'm being punished, but it's not as bad as you might think."

When Meuccio pressed him about punishment for sleeping with his godson's mother, Tingoccio shared the most hilarious detail: Apparently, in the afterlife, nobody cares about hooking up with your "gossip" (godchild's parent). His supernatural buddy basically told him, "Don't sweat it, dude."

Hearing this, Meuccio realized he'd been way too cautious in his own romantic pursuits. From that moment on, he decided to live a little differently.

And that's how a supernatural encounter taught one man to be more adventurous in love.
