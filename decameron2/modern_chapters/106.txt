The Tenth Story: A Magical Mishap

Let me tell you a wild story about what happens when you try to mess with magic—and don't follow the instructions exactly.

In a small town in southern Italy, there was a priest named Father Gianni who didn't make much money. To make ends meet, he would travel around selling things at local markets, bringing along his trusty mare. During his travels, he became close friends with Pietro, another traveling merchant who had an old donkey.

Whenever Father Gianni visited Pietro's hometown, Pietro would invite him to stay at his tiny house. They had a tight friendship, sharing what little they had. The only problem was that Pietro's house was super small, with just one bed that he shared with his young, attractive wife. So whenever Father Gianni visited, he'd end up sleeping next to his mare in their tiny stable.

Pietro's wife knew how hospitable Father Gianni was to her husband in his own town. She'd even suggested sleeping at a neighbor's house so Father Gianni could share the bed with her husband. But Father Gianni always refused.

One day, Father Gianni made a crazy claim. He told Pietro's wife, "Don't worry about me. Whenever I want, I can turn my mare into a beautiful woman and sleep with her. Then, when I'm done, I can turn her back into a mare!"

The wife was stunned—and intrigued. She told her husband, "Why don't you learn this magical trick? Imagine if you could turn me into a mare when we need to do farm work, then change me back afterwards?"

Pietro, who wasn't the sharpest tool in the shed, thought this was a brilliant idea. He begged Father Gianni to teach him the transformation spell.

After much pestering, Father Gianni agreed. "Fine," he said. "I'll show you how to do this. But you've got to follow my instructions exactly."

Early the next morning, Father Gianni began the magical transformation. He had Pietro's wife strip down and stand on all fours. He started chanting and touching different parts of her body, turning her "head into a mare's head" and her "legs into mare's legs."

Just as he was about to add the final touch—the tail—Pietro got nervous and shouted, "Wait! I don't want a tail there!"

And just like that, the entire magical spell was ruined.

Father Gianni was furious. "I told you not to say a word!" he yelled. "The transformation was perfect until you interrupted!"

Pietro's wife was devastated. "You idiot!" she told her husband. "What mare have you ever seen without a tail?"

And so, the magical transformation failed, the wife remained human, and they went back to their normal lives—Pietro still traveling with his donkey, just like before.

The moral of the story? Always read the instruction manual—especially when it comes to magic!
