"I care more about your happiness than about getting married," Gisippus told him. "If you truly love Sophronia, she's yours. Our friendship matters more than any arranged marriage."

Titus was shocked. Who gives up their fiancée for a friend? But Gisippus was serious. He was willing to step aside completely, proving that true friendship means putting your friend's happiness before your own.

What happened next? Well, that's a story that shows how extraordinary human beings can be when they truly love each other—not just romantically, but as friends.
