The Tenth Story: A Wild Night of Mistaken Identity

Dioneo looked around at his companions, sensing their exhaustion from the day's sad love stories. "I think we could use something a bit more entertaining," he said with a mischievous grin. "Let me tell you about a night that went seriously wrong in Salerno."

Here's what happened: There was this famous doctor named Dr. Mazzeo, who was pretty old but had married a young, beautiful woman. Despite showering her with fancy clothes and jewels, he was terrible at being a husband. He barely spent any time with her and made up ridiculous excuses about needing days to "recover" after being intimate.

Unsurprisingly, his wife wasn't thrilled about this arrangement. She decided if her husband wasn't going to give her the attention she deserved, she'd find someone who would. Enter Ruggieri da Jeroli - a guy with a sketchy reputation but who caught her eye.

Ruggieri was known around town as a troublemaker - someone who'd stolen so much that he'd burned every social bridge. But the doctor's wife didn't care. With her maid's help, they started a secret relationship.

One night, everything went completely sideways. Dr. Mazzeo had prepared a special sleeping potion for a patient with a leg injury and was called away suddenly to Malfi. His wife, seeing her chance, invited Ruggieri over. But when Ruggieri got thirsty, he accidentally drank the doctor's medical potion, thinking it was water.

The result? Ruggieri passed out cold. His lover thought he was dead. Panicking, she and her maid decided to hide his body in a nearby chest. But here's where things get crazy - two local money lenders stole that very chest, with Ruggieri still inside!

When Ruggieri finally woke up, he was completely confused and found himself in the money lenders' house. The neighbors heard a commotion, assumed he was a thief, and called the police. Suddenly, Ruggieri was facing potential execution.

But his clever lover and her even more clever maid hatched a plan. The maid went to the authorities and spun an elaborate story that not only saved Ruggieri but got the money lenders in trouble for stealing the chest.

In the end, Ruggieri was freed, the money lenders were fined, and the lovers continued their relationship - laughing about their absolutely insane night.

"And that," Dioneo concluded with a wink, "is why you should always check what you're drinking."
