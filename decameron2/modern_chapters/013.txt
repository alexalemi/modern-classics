Day Two

The Start of Another Storytelling Adventure

The morning sunlight was already spreading across the landscape, and birds were singing playfully among the green branches, their cheerful melodies announcing the new day. The ladies and three young men rose early, wandering into the gardens. They walked slowly through the dewy grass, their steps gentle, weaving flower garlands and entertaining themselves.

Just like the previous day, they followed their usual routine: enjoying a cool meal, dancing for a while, then taking a short rest. After their afternoon break, they gathered in the meadow as their queen had requested, settling in a circle around her.

She sat there, looking charming and wonderfully pleasant, wearing her laurel crown. After taking a moment to look at everyone, she asked Neifile to begin the day's stories with one of her choosing. Without hesitation, Neifile happily started to speak.
