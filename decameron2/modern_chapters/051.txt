The Fourth Story: A Clever Path to Love

Love has a way of finding its path, even when parents think they're watching carefully. This is the story of Ricciardo and Caterina - two young people who were definitely not going to let a little thing like strict parental supervision get in their way.

Messer Lizio da Valbona was an older gentleman who had one cherished daughter, Caterina. She was beautiful, spirited, and - as her parents would soon discover - incredibly clever. Living in the countryside of Romagna, her parents had high hopes of making an excellent marriage match for her.

Enter Ricciardo, a handsome young man from the Manardi family. He had been a family friend for so long that Messer Lizio and his wife treated him almost like another son. Almost.

Ricciardo had been watching Caterina for a while, totally head-over-heels in love but trying to keep it a secret. To his surprise and delight, Caterina had noticed him too - and she was just as interested.

One day, they managed a quick conversation where Ricciardo said, "I'm dying of love for you," and Caterina instantly replied, "And I might die if you don't do something about it!"

They needed a plan. Caterina was watched like a hawk, but she was determined. She came up with a brilliant scheme involving the gallery next to her father's garden. Her pitch to Ricciardo was simple: "If you can find a way to meet me there, I'll make it happen."

The opportunity came during a warm May evening. Caterina started complaining about the heat to her mother, suggesting she might sleep more comfortably in the gallery - conveniently located near her parents' bedroom. After some negotiation, her parents agreed.

That night, Ricciardo climbed the walls - literally - and made his way to Caterina. They spent a passionate night together, thinking they were being incredibly clever.

But parents are always one step ahead.

The next morning, Messer Lizio discovered them together. Instead of exploding with anger, he saw an opportunity. He told Ricciardo he could marry Caterina and avoid serious trouble - or face much worse consequences.

Ricciardo didn't hesitate. They were married right then and there, with Caterina's parents present. A few days later, they had a proper wedding celebration with all their friends and family.

And that's how a nighttime adventure in the gallery became a happily-ever-after love story.
