The Sixth Story: A Night of Misunderstandings

Let me tell you about a wild night that proves love makes people do some pretty crazy things.

Once everyone seemed asleep, Pinuccio snuck over to his girlfriend's bed. They were enjoying some private time together when suddenly, a noise from a cat startled the innkeeper's wife. She got up in the dark to investigate.

Things got even more complicated when Pinuccio, finished with his girlfriend, accidentally climbed into bed with the innkeeper, thinking it was Adriano's bed. He started bragging about his romantic adventures, not realizing he was talking to the girl's father!

The innkeeper was furious, but the wife - being incredibly quick-witted - immediately covered for everyone. She claimed Pinuccio was just talking in his sleep and made up the whole story.

By morning, everyone played it cool. Pinuccio and Adriano left town, the innkeeper thought it was just a weird dream, and his wife knew exactly what had happened - but wasn't about to tell anyone.

Sometimes, a little mischief and a lot of quick thinking can turn a potentially scandalous situation into nothing more than a funny story.
