Translation Notes — Chapter 033 (Tome 3 Front Matter)

CONTEXT:
This is the title page and front matter for Tome 3 of the original French edition. In the original publishing, Tocqueville's two-volume work was issued in multiple physical tomes. Tome 3 begins Volume II (the 1840 volume). This is publisher's information — printer, address, date, edition number.

KEY TRANSLATION DECISIONS:

1. "Membre de l'Institut" → "Member of the Institut." Left "Institut" in French as it refers specifically to the Institut de France, the prestigious French learned society. Translating it as "the Institute" would lose the specific reference.

2. "Avertissement" in the subtitle → "Preface." Consistent with the decision in chapter 001 (the Volume I preface).

3. "Examen comparatif de la Démocratie aux États-Unis et en Suisse" → "Comparative Study of Democracy in the United States and in Switzerland." This appendix, which Tocqueville added in later editions, is not included in most source texts but is referenced on this title page.

4. Omitted the printer's information (PARIS.--IMPRIMERIE CLAYE ET TAILLEFER / RUE SAINT-BENOIT, 7) as it is purely a production detail from the original print edition.

5. Omitted the final repeated title "DE LA DEMOCRATIE EN AMERIQUE" that appears at the end — this is a running header/section marker from the original print layout.

TONE:
Straightforward bibliographic translation. No interpretive decisions needed.

NEXT CHAPTER NOTES:
- Chapter 034 is the Avertissement (Preface) to Volume II, where Tocqueville explains the purpose and scope of this second volume. His tone will be personal and methodological.
