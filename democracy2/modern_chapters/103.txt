PART FOUR.

ON THE INFLUENCE THAT DEMOCRATIC IDEAS AND FEELINGS EXERCISE ON POLITICAL SOCIETY.


I would be failing in the purpose of this book if, after showing the ideas and feelings that equality inspires, I did not conclude by showing what general influence these same feelings and ideas can have on the governance of human societies.

To do this, I will often have to retrace my steps. But I trust the reader will not refuse to follow me when familiar paths lead to some new truth.
